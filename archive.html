Daily Verbage

Consumation | 8.16.18

(noun)

Definition

1: the act of consummating : the consummation of a contract by mutual signature : specifically : the consummating of a marriage.

2: the point at which something is complete or finalized.

Synonyms

completion, culmination, finish, realization, end, fulfilment.

Cacophony : In Sentances

"After the long journey, the two finally consumated their marriage and lived togethor for the end of their days."

"The relentless filing had come to an end, offering officer Shaw the relief of having consumated the case."
